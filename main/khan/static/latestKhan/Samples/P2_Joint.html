The denominator for the joint probability is the total number of patients.

The denominator for the conditional probability is the number of people who have lymphoma.

Make sure that you have the right numerators for both probabilities.

P(female, lymphoma) = \frac{10}{569} = 0.018

P(female | lymphoma) = \frac{10}{59 + 10} = 0.145
